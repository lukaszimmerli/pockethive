Privacy Policy

Uncover

Last updated: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Uncover ("the App"), developed by PocketHive ("we," "our," or "us"), handles your information. We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

The Short Version:

We don't collect or store any personal information about you. The App uses third-party services (Mixpanel for analytics and Unity Ads for advertising) that may collect certain data to help us improve the game and show you relevant ads.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information about you. This includes:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No account creation or login required
  • No photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • No location data

1.2 Game Data

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

1.3 Analytics Data (Mixpanel)

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how players interact with the game and improve the experience. This includes:

  • App events (e.g., level started, level completed, game features used)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Anonymous device identifier

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. For more information about Mixpanel's data practices, please visit: Mixpanel Privacy Policy

1.4 Advertising Data (Unity Ads)

The App displays advertisements through Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect and process certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address (used for general location, not precise location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Unity Ads uses this information to show you relevant advertisements. For more information about Unity Ads' data practices, please visit: Unity Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous analytics data collected through Mixpanel is used solely to:

  • Understand which game features are most enjoyed
  • Identify and fix bugs or technical issues
  • Improve game balance and difficulty progression
  • Make informed decisions about future updates and features

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties except for the analytics and advertising services mentioned above.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

Used for app analytics and performance monitoring.

View Mixpanel Privacy Policy →

Unity Ads (Advertising)

Used to display advertisements within the App.

4. Your Choices

4.1 Limit Ad Tracking

You can limit ad tracking on your iOS device:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Or manage tracking permissions per app

4.2 Reset Advertising Identifier

You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings to limit ad personalization.

4.3 Delete Game Data

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it completely by uninstalling the App.

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any personal information on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being compromised through our systems. All game data is stored securely on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. The data collected by Mixpanel and Unity Ads is subject to their respective security practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Uncover is appropriate for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The anonymous analytics and advertising data collected through third-party services is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

7. International Data Transfers

The App is available worldwide. The analytics data collected by Mixpanel and advertising data collected by Unity Ads may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where these services operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, traditional data access, correction, or deletion requests do not apply to data we control. However:

  • For analytics data collected by Mixpanel, you can contact Mixpanel directly or opt-out at: https://mixpanel.com/optout/
  • For advertising data collected by Unity Ads, you can manage your preferences through your device settings or contact Unity directly
  • You can delete all local game data by uninstalling the App

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights. Please contact us if you have questions about exercising these rights.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing a notice within the App if changes materially affect data collection

Your continued use of the App after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
